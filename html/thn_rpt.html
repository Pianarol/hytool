thn_rpt

Produces the final figure and results for the Theis model with a no flow boundary

Syntax

thn_rpt( p, t, s, d, ttle )
 
    p = parameters of the model
    t = measured time
    s = measured drawdown
    d(1) = Q = Pumping rate
    d(2) = r = Distance between the observation and the pumping well 
    ttle = Title of the figure (Optional)

Description

    Produces the final figure and results for Theis model (1935) with 
    a no flow boundary.

See Also

thn_dmo, thn_dim, thn_gss
