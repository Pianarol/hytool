HYTOOL Function Reference

csl_rpt

Produces the final figure and results for the Papadopulos Cooper model

Syntax

csl_rpt( p, t, s, ttle )
 
    p = parameters of the Papadopulos-Cooper model
    t = measured time
    s = measured drawdown
    ttle = Title of the figure

Description

    Produces the final figure and results for the Papadopulos Cooper 
    model (1967).
 
  WARNING: to use CSL_RPT, one needs first to call CS_PRE to define the 
           variables that are required for the interpretation.
 
     Reference: Cooper, H.H.J., J.D. Bredehoeft, and I.S. Papadopulos.
     1967. Response of a finite-diameter well to an instantaneous charge of
     water. Water Resources Research 3, no. 1: 263-269.
