pcb_rpt

Produces the final figure and results for the Papadopulos Cooper model with independent wellbore storage

Syntax

pcb_rpt(p,t,s,ttle )
 
    p(1) = a  = slope of the late time straight line
    p(2) = t0 = intersept of late time straight line
    t = measured time
    s = measured drawdown
    ttle = Title of the figure

Description

    Produces the final figure and results for the Papadopulos-Cooper model
    with independent wellbore storage

See Also

pcb_dmo, pcb_pre, pcb_dim, pcb_gss
