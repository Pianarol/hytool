HYTOOL Function Reference

thc_dls

Theis dimensionless drawdown with a constant head boundary

Syntax

s = thc_dls(p,t)
 
    p(1) = r1/r2  
    with r1 = radius to the pumping well 
    and  r2 = radius to the image well 
 
    provides the dimensionless drawdown at reduced time t

See Also

thc_dim, thc_lap
