aga_rpt

Produces the final figure and results for the Agarwal model

Syntax

aga_rpt(p,t,s,ttle )
 
    p(1) = a  = slope of the late time straight line
    p(2) = t0 = intersept of late time straight line
    p(3) = sg = sigma, coefficient de skin
    t = measured time
    s = measured drawdown
    ttle = Title of the figure

Description

    Produces the final figure and results for the Agarwal model (1970).

See Also

aga_dmo, aga_pre, aga_gss, aga_dim
