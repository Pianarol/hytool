aga_lap

Agarwal et al solution in Laplace domain

Syntax

aga_lap(x,p) 
 
    x(1) = Cd
    x(2) = rd
    x(3) = sigma
    p    = Laplace parameter
 
    calculates the dimensionless drawdown in the aquifer
