htj_rpt

Produces the final figure and results for the Hantush and Jacob (1955) model

Syntax

hcj_rpt( p, t, s, d, ttle )
 
    p(1) = a  = slope of the late time straight line
    p(2) = t0 = intersept of late time straight line
    p(3) = r/B 
    t = measured time
    s = measured drawdown
    d(1) = Q = Pumping rate
    d(2) = r = Distance to the pumping well
    d(3) = e' = Thickness of the aquitard
    ttle = Title of the figure

Description

    Produces the final figure and results for Hantush (1955) model.

See Also

htj_dmo, htj_dim, htj_gss
